Institutionc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://lod.ehri-project-test.eu/ontology#Institution

This class is a specialisation of the rico:CorporateBody and ehri:CorporateBody classes, taking some additional properties from the schema:ArchiveOrganization class. By contrast to a Corporate Body, in EHRI's data model, an Institution always hold archival records which is in line with ISDIAH definition.
has super-classes
Archive Organization c, Corporate body c
is in domain of
accessibility dp, administrative structure dp, building(s) dp, conditions of access dp, finding aids dp, general context dp, has copy op, is copy of op, public areas dp, records management and collecting policies dp, reproduction services dp, research services dp
is in range of
has copy op, is copy of op
