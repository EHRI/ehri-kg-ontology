Introduction back to ToC

The EHRI Portal is an aggregator for descriptions of archival material relating to the Holocaust, based around International Council on Archives (ICA) conceptual models such as [ISAD(G)], [ISAAR (CPF)] and [ISDIAH], with some adaptations to cater to its specific use case (see the EHRI Portal data model in Figure 1). With the release of [RiC-O], EHRI is seeking to explore the benefits this new model can bring to both the EHRI Portal and collection-holding institutions alike [EHRI-KG]. This ontology is an adaptation of EHRI's existing data model to RiC-O axioms, including attributes derived from the aforementioned conceptual models, with the aim of enabling a more straightforward translation from those widely-adopted standards.
